Our Focus

Excellence in Hospitality & Real Estate

Regal Heights is a multi-faceted brand offering primarily luxurious short-let accommodations in the city of London. Situated in the Royal Victoria area at the foot of the Excel Conference Centre, we host corporate, business, and leisure travellers alike.

We actively encourage booking directly with us to guarantee the most competitive rates and an unparalleled level of service. Direct communication ensures absolute flexibility, tailored fulfillment of special requests, and even real-time WhatsApp video viewings of our apartments prior to your arrival.

Beyond our distinguished hospitality, we operate as a discreet London-based referral and introductions firm. We connect qualified UK and international buyers to prime property markets in iconic cities like London, Dubai, New York, and Paris.

Disclaimer: We are not a property sales agency and do not handle sales funds on property referrals. We facilitate access through screening and introduction to certified real estate partners for a commissionable fee.
